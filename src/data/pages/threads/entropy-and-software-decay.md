# entropy and software decay

physics dictates that closed systems move towards disorder. software is no different.

![decaying structure](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1596564639735-0ae099684980?q=80&w=800&auto=format&fit=crop)

dependencies update, hardware changes, and human understanding of the original codebase fades. this is the "bit rot".

maintenance is not just fixing bugs; it is the active injection of energy to counteract entropy.
